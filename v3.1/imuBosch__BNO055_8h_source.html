37 /* Serial protocol description
38  *
39  * Write operation on a register:
40  * | Byte 1 | Byte 2 | Byte 3 | Byte 4 | Byte 5 | ... | Byte (n+4) |
41  * | Start | Write | Reg Addr| Length | Data 1 | ... | Data n |
42  * | 0xAA | 0x00 | <...> | <...> | <...> | ... | <...> |
43  *
44  * Response to write command:
45  * | 0xEE | <code> |
46  * 0x01: Write_success
47  * all other values are errors ... TODO: improve doc
48  *
49  * Read operation on a register:
50  * | Byte 1 | Byte 2 | Byte 3 | Byte 4 |
51  * | Start | Read | Reg Addr| Length |
52  * | 0xAA | 0x01 | <...> | <...> |
53  *
54  * Response to a successful read command:
55  * | Byte 1 | Byte 2 | Byte 3 | ... | Byte (n+2) |
56  * | Resp | Length | Data 1 | ... | Data n |
57  * | 0xBB | <...> | <...> | ... | <...> |
58  *
59  * Response to a failed read command:
60  * | Byte 1 | Byte 2 |
61  * | Resp | Status |
62  * | 0xEE | <...> |
63  *
64  * Read error code: TODO
65  */
